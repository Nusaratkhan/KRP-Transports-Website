About Us

Established in 2002, KRP Transports is a trusted logistics partner specializing in the safe and compliant transportation of Bulk LPG across India. With over two decades of experience, we support the nation’s energy supply chain through disciplined operations and reliable service delivery.

Our commitment to safety, regulatory compliance, and operational excellence has earned us long-standing partnerships with leading PSU oil companies such as BPCL and IOCL.

Nationwide Operations

Our fleet of PESO-approved LPG tankers operates across key regions in India, ensuring timely and secure deliveries through disciplined operations.

Zero-Accident Safety Culture

Safety is embedded into every operation through strict training, audits, and emergency response protocols.

Reliable & Compliant Handling
